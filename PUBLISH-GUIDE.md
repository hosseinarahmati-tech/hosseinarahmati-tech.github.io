# How to Publish Your Website (Plain-Language Guide)

Your website is finished and ready. Follow these steps to put it online for free with
GitHub Pages. No coding needed — just a web browser.

**Your folder:** `/Users/amirrahmati/hossein-rahmati-website`
**Everything in it gets uploaded.**

---

## Step 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to **https://github.com/signup**
2. Sign up using your email address.
3. When it asks for a **username**, choose **`hosseinrahmati`** if it's free.
   - This matters: your website address will be **https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io**
   - So `hosseinrahmati` gives you `https://hosseinrahmati.github.io` ✅
   - If that username is taken, pick another and **tell me** — I'll update the website's
     SEO links to match (5-minute fix).

## Step 2 — Create the website repository
1. Click the **+** (top right) → **New repository**
2. **Repository name:** type exactly `YOURUSERNAME.github.io`
   (for example `hosseinrahmati.github.io`)
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

## Step 3 — Upload the website files
1. On the new empty repository page, click **"uploading an existing file"**
   (a blue link in the middle of the page).
2. Open the folder `hossein-rahmati-website` on your Mac.
3. Select **everything inside it** (the `index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`,
   `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt` files **and** the `images` folder) and drag it
   onto the GitHub upload page.
4. Scroll down and click **Commit changes**.

## Step 4 — Turn on GitHub Pages
1. In your repository, click **Settings** (top menu).
2. On the left, click **Pages**.
3. Under "Branch", choose **main** and **/ (root)**, then click **Save**.
4. Wait 1–2 minutes.

## Step 5 — Your site is live! 🎉
Visit **https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io** — your website is now public.

---

## Step 6 — Help Google find you (SEO)
1. Go to **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Sign in with your Google account.
3. Add your site address as a **URL prefix** property: `https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io`
4. Verify (the easiest method is the "HTML tag" — paste me the tag and I'll add it,
   or follow Google's on-screen steps).
5. Once verified, go to **Sitemaps** and submit: `sitemap.xml`

That tells Google to index your site so people searching **"Hossein Rahmati"** can find it.
